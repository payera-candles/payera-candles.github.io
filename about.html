Different Types of Waxes

Currently we use a vareity of waxes to making candle - the very popular ones are Paraffin and Soy, but there are other options like Palm, Coconut, Beeswax and Specialty Plant Waxes (e.g., Carnauba, Candelilla, Japan wax)

Let's understand why we use each of them to make our candles

  • Paraffin is the cheapest wax available, hence it is used by a large number of retailers, but this produces a whole lot of toxins as it is made of petroleum
  • Soy wax is made out of Soybean oil, less harmful for the environment, it costs a bit more than paraffin wax
  • Coconut wax is extremely eco-friendly as it is the most natural wax form, but it is too expensive (due to its low supply) to use as a raw material for a candle maker
