Why We Choose These Materials — Quality, Safety & Sustainability

At Payera, every element of our candles is chosen thoughtfully. We want you to enjoy beautiful, long-lasting light without compromise. Here's a closer look at what goes into each jar:

Sustainable Soy Wax

We use 100% natural soy wax, derived from renewable soybeans. Compared to traditional paraffin (a petroleum byproduct), soy wax offers several advantages:

  • Cleaner burn — Produces minimal soot when properly wicked and trimmed, helping keep your walls and ceilings cleaner.
  • Longer-lasting — Soy wax has a lower melting point, so it burns slower and more evenly — often 30-50% longer than paraffin candles of the same size.
  • Eco-friendly — Biodegradable, renewable, and supports agriculture (especially when sourced responsibly).
  • Excellent scent throw — Holds fragrance oils beautifully for a strong yet natural aroma experience.

Note: Reputable sources like the National Candle Association confirm that all major candle waxes (soy, paraffin, beeswax, etc.) can burn cleanly and safely when made properly — the key is quality ingredients and proper care.

Pure Cotton Wicks

We only use 100% natural cotton wicks (lead-free and zinc-free) because safety and clean burning are non-negotiable.

  • Even, stable flame — Reduces mushrooming and excessive flickering.
  • Minimal soot & smoke — Especially important for indoor air quality.
  • Safer — No harmful heavy metals or synthetic cores that were once common in cheaper wicks.

Responsible Burning & Fire Safety

We love candles, but safety comes first. Follow these simple guidelines from experts like the National Candle Association:

  • Always trim the wick to ¼ inch (6mm) before lighting — prevents high flames and soot.
  • Never leave a burning candle unattended.
  • Keep candles at least 12 inches (30 cm) away from anything flammable (curtains, books, bedding).
  • Place on a stable, heat-resistant surface, out of reach of children and pets.
  • Avoid drafts — they cause uneven burning and smoking.

With proper care, your Payera candle will bring joy and calm — safely.
